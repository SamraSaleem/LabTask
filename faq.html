Frequently asked question

  • What is the Galactic Odyssey Expo?

    The Galactic Odyssey Expo is a celebration of science fiction, featuring immersive experiences, panels with renowned authors, and showcases of futuristic technology.

  • Where and when is the event held?

    The event will take place at the Cosmic Nexus Arena in Neo Metropolis from October 5-7, 2024.

  • Who can attend the Galactic Odyssey Expo?

    The expo is open to all science fiction enthusiasts, including fans, creators, and professionals. There are activities and panels suitable for all ages.
